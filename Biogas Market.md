<p data-start="546" data-end="1092"><strong data-start="546" data-end="567">Market Definition</strong>:<br data-start="568" data-end="571" />Biogas is a renewable energy source produced through the anaerobic digestion of organic matter such as agricultural waste, food waste, and sewage. The biogas production process involves the breakdown of organic materials by bacteria in the absence of oxygen, resulting in a mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide. Biogas is used in various applications, including electricity generation, heating, and as a fuel for vehicles, making it an important component in the shift toward renewable energy solutions.</p>
