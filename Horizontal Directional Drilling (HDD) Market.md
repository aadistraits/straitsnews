HDD has become an increasingly popular solution for the installation of various utilities, as it minimizes surface disruption and is especially effective for drilling under roads, rivers, and other obstacles. The method offers a reliable and efficient way of completing underground drilling projects, with reduced environmental impact compared to traditional methods. HDD&rsquo;s versatility, ability to minimize surface disturbance, and cost-effectiveness are key factors driving its increasing adoption across industries such as oil &amp; gas, telecommunications, and utilities.</p>
