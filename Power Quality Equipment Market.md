<p data-start="667" data-end="688"><strong data-start="667" data-end="688">Market Definition</strong></p>
<p data-start="690" data-end="1326">Power quality equipment is used to improve the quality of power supply by correcting various issues such as voltage fluctuations, harmonics, and electrical noise. These issues can impact the performance of electrical devices and can cause system failures in both industrial and commercial sectors. Power quality equipment includes voltage regulators, power conditioners, uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems, surge protectors, and filters, among others. These devices are essential in maintaining the efficiency and reliability of electrical systems, ensuring the optimal functioning of sensitive electronic devices and machinery.</p>
