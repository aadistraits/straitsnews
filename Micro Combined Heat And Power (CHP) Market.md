<p data-start="591" data-end="1133"><strong data-start="591" data-end="612">Market Definition</strong>:<br data-start="613" data-end="616" /><strong><a href="https://straitsresearch.com/report/micro-combined-heat-and-power-market">Micro Combined Heat and Power (CHP)</a></strong> is a technology that simultaneously produces electricity and useful heat from a single energy source. The heat generated from power generation is captured and utilized for heating purposes in residential, commercial, or industrial buildings. CHP systems are highly efficient and can contribute to reducing energy costs, improving energy security, and decreasing greenhouse gas emissions. These systems can operate on various fuels such as natural gas, biomass, and even waste heat.</p>
