These ingredients are an essential part of culinary traditions worldwide. Spices are derived from the seeds, fruits, roots, and bark of plants, while seasonings can include a variety of herbs, blends, and artificial flavoring agents. Both seasonings and spices are used in different quantities, depending on the dish, and can range from common varieties like black pepper and cumin to more exotic options like saffron and cardamom.</p>
