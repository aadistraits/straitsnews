<p data-start="626" data-end="647"><strong data-start="626" data-end="647">Market Definition</strong></p>
<p data-start="649" data-end="1056">Toothpaste is a gel or paste used in conjunction with a toothbrush to clean and maintain the health of teeth. It works by helping to remove food particles and prevent the buildup of plaque, which can lead to cavities and other oral diseases. Toothpaste also contains fluoride, which helps to strengthen teeth and prevent decay, along with other ingredients that aid in freshening breath and whitening teeth.</p>
