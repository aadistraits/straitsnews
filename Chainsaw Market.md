<p data-start="797" data-end="1295">A chainsaw is a portable power tool used for cutting through wood, typically with a set of rotating teeth attached to a chain. It is widely used in a variety of applications, including forestry, landscaping, and construction. The chainsaw market encompasses different types of chainsaws, such as gas-powered, electric, and battery-operated models. The tool is employed by professionals in industries such as forestry and agriculture, as well as by homeowners for landscaping and tree-cutting needs.</p>
